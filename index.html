Chanjin Park

I am a robotics researcher and M.S. candidate at Yonsei University, specializing in generative model–based imitation learning and bio-signal–driven robotic systems.

Throughout my master's program, I have studied optimization, reinforcement learning, and linear systems, and have conducted research on enabling robots to reproduce human behaviors through imitation learning. In particular, I am highly interested in generative-model approaches that allow robots to generate and track both positional and force trajectories.

Moving forward, I aim to continue advancing imitation learning methods that can robustly generate both motion and force, ultimately enabling robots to solve complex real-world tasks that are currently difficult to perform.
